The Plane

Pune: The Bishop’s School, Camp, acquired an MiG-21 aircraft , which the institute will unveil on Friday evening.The school’s principal Joel Edwin said the aircraft were acquired to motivate students to look at a career in the Armed forces.The event will be attended by Maj Gen Navneet Kumar, General Officer Commanding, Dakshin Maharashtra Sub Area, Pune, and Lt Gen A B Shivane (retd), Director General, Mechanised Forces, officials of Pune Cantonment Board, Air Cdre Rahul Bhasin VM, Air Officer Commanding, 2 Wing Air Force Station, Pune and the school’s governing body.
